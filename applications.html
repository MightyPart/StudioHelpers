Mod Application

Apply to become a part of our Mod team. If you are accepted you will be trialed for one week as a Trial Mod before being promoted to Mod. All promoted mods will get Helpers+ permanantly unless demoted or quit.

Certification Application

Apply to become Certified on Studio Helpers. Certification grants you access to features in the server that will help you, your group or your game studio. Features incude; yellow ribbon when sending marketplace requests, access to your own personal devlog ping, Helpers+, access to certified only channels and more.

VerDev Application

Apply to become recognised for your field in roblox development. You get the corresponding role(s), access to VerDev only channels, Access to stream video in any vc and VerDev badge in profile.
